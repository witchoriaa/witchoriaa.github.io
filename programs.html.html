Our Programs

Programs We Offer

Our educational programs are designed to inspire a lifelong love for learning.

Erasmus+

Erasmus+ is a European Union (EU) program that supports education, training, youth, and sport activities in Europe. It aims to promote mobility, cooperation, and the exchange of knowledge and skills among individuals and organizations across the EU and beyond. Erasmus+ encompasses various sub-programs and actions

Fulbright Program

As mentioned earlier, the Fulbright Program is one of the most prominent exchange programs in the USA. It offers a variety of opportunities for students, scholars, and professionals to study, teach, conduct research, and engage in cultural exchange in the United States.

JET

The JET Program is one of the most well-known exchange programs in Japan. It recruits English speakers from various countries to work as Assistant Language Teachers (ALTs) in Japanese schools, or as Coordinators for International Relations (CIRs) in local government offices. Participants help teach English and promote cultural exchange.

Budapesti Műszaki és Gazdaságtudományi Egyetem

The Budapesti Műszaki és Gazdaságtudományi Egyetem (Budapest University of Technology and Economics or abbreviated as BME) is one of the leading universities in Hungary. The university is located in Budapest, the capital city of Hungary. BME has a history of more than 200 years and plays a prominent role in the field of education and research in Hungary, especially in the fields of engineering and economics.

Georgetown University

Georgetown University is a prestigious private research university located in Washington, D.C., United States. It is one of the oldest Catholic universities in the country and is known for its strong academic programs, especially in international relations, law, business, and foreign service.It's important to note that Georgetown University is highly competitive, and admission to the university can be challenging.

The University of Tokyo

The University of Tokyo, often referred to as "Todai" (short for Tokyo Daigaku in Japanese), is Japan's most prestigious and one of Asia's leading universities. It is widely recognized for its excellence in education and research across various disciplines.The University of Tokyo's global reputation and academic excellence make it a highly sought-after institution for both Japanese and international students.
